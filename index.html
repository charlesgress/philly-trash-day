Common Questions

Why is my collection day showing later than usual?

When a City-observed holiday falls on or before your normal collection day, the rest of that week shifts by one day. If the holiday is on a Friday, Friday pickups move to Saturday.

I have trash collected twice a week — does that change?

Yes. During a holiday week, twice-weekly areas receive only one pickup, on the same day as recycling. The secondary trash day won't run that week.

Good to Know

🗓 Weekend holidays (like July 4th this year) don't affect the following week's collection.

⚠ Two holidays in one week may cause delays beyond one day. Check phila.gov for the official modified schedule.
